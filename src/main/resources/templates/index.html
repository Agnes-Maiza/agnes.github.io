Our discussion today is about mental health

Not sure if you or someone you know is living with mental health problems? Experiencing one or more of the following feelings or behaviors can be an early warning sign of a problem:

  1. Eating or sleeping too much or too little
  2. Feeling numb or like nothing matters
  3. Having low or no energy
  4. Pulling away from people and usual activities
  5. Having unexplained aches and pains
  6. Feeling helpless or hopeless
  7. Feeling unusually confused, forgetful, on edge, angry, upset, worried, or scared
  8. Experiencing severe mood swings that cause problems in relationships
  9. Yelling or fighting with family and friends
  10. Smoking, drinking, or using drugs more than usual
  11. Having persistent thoughts and memories you can't get out of your head
  12. Thinking of harming yourself or others
  13. Hearing voices or believing things that are not true
  14. Inability to perform daily tasks like taking care of your kids or getting to work or school

If you have any of the above symptoms feel free to get help
